Governance Structure

Governance Authority

Governance authority resides with [Company Name] Executive leadership, the Data Protection Officer, Information Security, and Engineering.

Material exceptions and residual risks require Executive or DPO approval as specified in this document.

Privacy Steering Forum

Charter

The Privacy Steering Forum is the primary decision body for material privacy matters affecting [Product Name].

Forum MUST meet at least quarterly with DPO, Security, Engineering, Product, and Operations representation.

Ad-hoc sessions MUST be convened within 48 hours for high-severity privacy or AI safety events.

Decision register

Material privacy changes MUST be logged in a privacy decision register with owner, date, and outcome.

Open actions from forum MUST not exceed 90 days without Executive escalation.

Forum minutes MUST be published within five working days and retained per retention requirements.

Governance Controls

Mandatory controls

AI features MUST receive review against AI Governance Policy and AI Auditability Standard before production enablement.

Customer controller obligations MUST be reflected in DPA, Subprocessor List, and external privacy notices.

Privacy KPIs (DSR volume, breach count, training completion, open DPIAs) MUST be reported to Executive leadership semi-annually.

Conflicts between speed-to-market and privacy requirements MUST be resolved by DPO recommendation and Executive decision where unresolved.

Delegated authorities for privacy sign-off MUST be documented; acting approvers require written DPO delegation.

Regulatory and Audit Coordination

Mandatory controls

Regulatory horizon changes (ICO guidance, UK law) MUST be assessed within 30 days of publication with impact note to forum.

Cross-functional privacy incidents MUST trigger ad-hoc forum within 48 hours when severity is high.

Third-party privacy audits or customer questionnaires MUST be coordinated by Compliance with single version-controlled responses.

Data Protection Impact Assessments in flight MUST be status-reviewed monthly until signed or closed.

Privacy governance MUST NOT approve production hosting outside UK South for customer personal data.

AI and Processing Oversight

Mandatory controls

New AI processing types MUST have forum visibility before production enablement.

Subprocessor changes affecting privacy MUST be presented to forum before customer notice publication.

Material logging or retention changes MUST be forum-reviewed before DPO privacy gate sign-off.

Annual governance effectiveness review MUST assess forum attendance, action closure rate, and audit findings.

Executive Accountability

Mandatory controls

Semi-annual KPI pack MUST be prepared for Executive with trend analysis and resource asks.

Critical privacy risk without agreed mitigation MUST escalate to Executive Sponsor within 24 hours.

Resource conflicts affecting privacy controls MUST be documented with Executive decision record.

Operational Procedure

The following procedure SHALL be executed for every in-scope event. Deviations require DPO approval.

1. DPO circulates quarterly agenda including ROPA changes, incidents, subprocessors, AI register, and regulatory updates.

2. Workstream owners present decision requests with options, impacts, and recommended controls.

3. Forum records approve/reject/defer with assigned owners and due dates in decision register.

4. DPO publishes minutes and opens tracking tickets for each action.

5. Product and Engineering confirm implementation evidence before closing privacy actions.

6. Semi-annual KPI pack prepared for Executive with trend analysis and resource asks.

7. Annual governance effectiveness review assesses forum attendance, action closure rate, and audit findings.

8. Ad-hoc sessions convened within 48 hours for high-severity privacy or AI safety events.

9. Delegated approvers confirmed in writing when DPO unavailable for time-bound decisions.

10. Customer diligence responses coordinated through Compliance with Legal review.

Exceptions and Deviations

### Exceptions MUST

be documented with business justification,

include compensating controls and risk assessment,

name an approval authority (DPO or Executive),

include an expiry date not exceeding 90 days unless renewed.

Expired exceptions SHALL be remediated, renewed, or formally closed within five working days of expiry.

Skipping a quarterly forum requires DPO and Executive written consent for that quarter only.
