Document purpose and scope

Article 30 record

This is the authoritative, comprehensive Article 30 UK GDPR record of processing for [Company Name] in connection with the [Product Name] platform and all related UK operations.

It covers every controller and processor activity identified as of June 2026, including planned processing (clearly marked), personnel, vendor relationships, and explicit exclusions.

RoPA entry has been created.

Security review has been completed.
