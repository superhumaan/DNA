Purpose

This agreement governs any transfer of personal data between the UK and Thailand entities within the [Company Name] group.

It addresses WorkNest gap analysis finding: employees in Thailand may have remote access to UK entity data without an intra-group agreement.

[Product Name] is offered exclusively in the United Kingdom. Customer data is processed and stored in Microsoft Azure UK South. [Company Name] does not offer multi-region tenancy or services outside the UK.

Customer workspace content ([Product Name] tenant data) is OUT OF SCOPE for routine Thailand access unless break-glass emergency with DPO approval.
