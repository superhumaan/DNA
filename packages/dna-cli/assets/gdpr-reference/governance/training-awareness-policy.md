Mandatory Training

Mandatory controls

All personnel with [Product Name] access MUST complete onboarding privacy and security training within 14 days before production access.

Annual refresher training MUST be completed by all in-scope personnel by calendar year-end.

Engineering MUST complete secure SDLC and tenant isolation module annually.

Support personnel MUST complete modules on no routine customer content access and DSR routing.

AI operators MUST complete AI Acceptable Use and in-memory pipeline overview annually.

Phishing and Effectiveness

Mandatory controls

Phishing simulations MUST run at least quarterly with clickers receiving remedial training within 10 working days.

Training content MUST be updated within 60 days of material policy or regulatory change.

New modules MUST be piloted with feedback before mandatory rollout.

Training records MUST include course version, date, and pass/fail where assessed.

Enforcement

Mandatory controls

Training completion below 95% MUST be reported to management monthly with named non-completers.

Persistent non-completion beyond 30 days MUST result in access suspension until complete.

Contractors MUST complete equivalent training before access; records retained by procurement.

Access provisioning blocked if onboarding training incomplete.

Leadership

Mandatory controls

Executive and board MUST receive annual privacy and security briefing.

Specialist roles (DPO deputies, incident responders) MUST complete advanced modules biennially.

Customers are not trained by this policy; customer admin guidance is separate.

Operational Procedure

The following procedure SHALL be executed for every in-scope event. Deviations require DPO approval.

1. HR assigns mandatory learning paths at joiner creation in LMS.

2. Security and DPO publish annual curriculum updates.

3. Managers monitor team completion weekly during annual campaign.

4. Compliance exports completion metrics monthly; escalates non-completers to HR.

5. Phishing campaign executed quarterly; remedial training auto-assigned to clickers.

6. Access provisioning blocked if onboarding training incomplete.

7. Annual attestation that personnel read key policies linked in LMS.

8. Year-end report to Executive with completion rate and improvement actions.

9. Role-specific modules assigned based on RBAC role.

10. Contractor training verified before procurement grants system access.

11. Curriculum version log updated within 60 days of policy changes.

12. Advanced modules scheduled biennially for incident responders.
