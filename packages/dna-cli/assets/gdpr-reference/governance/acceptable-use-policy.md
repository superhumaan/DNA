Permitted Use

Mandatory controls

IT systems MUST be used only for authorised business purposes related to [Product Name] and corporate operations.

Report security weaknesses to Security; exploitation without authorisation is prohibited.

Monitoring of corporate systems MUST be disclosed to personnel; expectation of privacy limited on business systems.

Prohibited Activities

Mandatory controls

Users MUST NOT attempt unauthorised access, port scanning, or circumvention of security controls on [Product Name] or corporate networks.

Customer personal data MUST NOT be downloaded to local devices except via approved support procedures with ticket and approval.

Prohibited content (illegal material, malware distribution, harassment) MUST NOT be stored or transmitted.

Cryptocurrency mining, peer-to-peer file sharing, and personal commercial use of production resources are prohibited.

Sharing administrator credentials or elevating personal privileges without approval is prohibited.

Data and AI

Mandatory controls

Personnel MUST NOT use personal email to transmit Confidential or Restricted data.

Use of unauthorised cloud storage for company data is prohibited.

Violation of AI Acceptable Use Policy while using corporate systems constitutes policy breach.

Social engineering of colleagues or customers is prohibited.

Enforcement

Mandatory controls

Investigations MUST preserve evidence with Legal and HR guidance.

Contractors MUST sign acceptable use before access; violations reported to procurement.

Disciplinary action up to termination and criminal referral for serious breaches.

Operational Procedure

The following procedure SHALL be executed for every in-scope event. Deviations require DPO approval.

1. HR collects electronic acknowledgement at onboarding and annually.

2. Security monitors corporate systems per Logging policy.

3. Suspected violation reported to Security with factual description.

4. Security investigates with HR; preserves logs and interviews.

5. Outcome: warning, access suspension, termination, or law enforcement referral via Legal.

6. Document closure in HR file and Security case log.

7. Annual communication reminds personnel of prohibitions and reporting channels.

8. Update policy when new threats (e.g., generative AI misuse) emerge.

9. Contractor violations reported to procurement with access revocation.

10. Criminal activity referred to Legal same day with evidence preservation.

11. Access suspension coordinated with IT within 24 hours for serious breaches.

12. Annual review of investigation outcomes for consistency.
