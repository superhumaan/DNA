GDPR Compliance Policy

## Uk Gdpr

Compliance framework

Objective

[Company Name] maintains a UK GDPR compliance programme for [Product Name] covering England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland.

[Product Name] is a workspace and productivity platform. It is not a medical device, clinical system, or regulated health record. Users must not rely on [Product Name] for diagnosis, treatment, or clinical decision-making. AI outputs are assistive drafts only.

[Product Name] is offered exclusively in the United Kingdom (England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland). Customer data is processed and stored in Microsoft Azure UK South. [Company Name] does not offer multi-region tenancy, data residency selection, or services outside the UK.

Requirements

Maintain ROPA for controller and [Product Name] processor activities (Article 30)

Conduct DPIAs for high-risk processing (Article 35), especially AI features

Document LIAs where legitimate interests are relied upon

Operate Data Subject Rights Procedure and Data Breach Response Procedure

ICO registration and fee where [Company Name] is controller

Annual management review and training for staff with [Product Name] access
