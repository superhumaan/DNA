# Government & Public Sector — Industry Overview

Government agencies need accessible, secure, auditable systems for citizens and civil servants.

**Typical products:** benefit portals, permit applications, case management, open data, procurement platforms.

**Jargon:** G-Cloud, FedRAMP, ATO, WCAG, VPAT, citizen identity, FOIA.

## When to install this pack

Citizen services, procurement portals, and regulated public systems

Run `dna plan industry gov-public-sector` to activate for this project.
