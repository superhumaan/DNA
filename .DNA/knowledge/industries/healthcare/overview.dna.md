# Healthcare — Industry Overview

Hospitals, clinics, payers, pharma, and digital health startups buy software for EHR integration, patient engagement, scheduling, billing, and population health.

**Typical products:** patient portals, clinician dashboards, telehealth, prior auth, RPM devices, practice management, clinical decision support.

**Jargon:** PHI, EHR/EMR, HL7, FHIR, CPT/ICD codes, prior authorization, care pathways, ADT feeds.

## When to install this pack

Clinical systems, patient portals, telehealth, and health data platforms

Run `dna plan industry healthcare` to activate for this project.
