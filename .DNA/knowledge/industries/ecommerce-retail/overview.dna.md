# E-commerce & Retail — Industry Overview

Brands and retailers need storefronts, inventory, fulfilment, loyalty, and personalisation at scale.

**Typical products:** D2C shops, B2B ordering portals, marketplace seller tools, POS integrations, loyalty apps.

**Jargon:** SKU, PDP, PLP, OMS, WMS, AOV, CAC, LTV, cart abandonment, omnichannel.

## When to install this pack

Online stores, marketplaces, omnichannel retail, and D2C brands

Run `dna plan industry ecommerce-retail` to activate for this project.
