# SaaS B2B — Industry Overview

B2B SaaS sells workflows to teams — CRM, HR, devtools, vertical ops, and platform APIs.

**Typical products:** admin dashboards, multi-tenant apps, integrations marketplaces, usage billing.

**Jargon:** ARR, MRR, churn, NRR, PLG, ICP, SSO, RBAC, tenant, seat-based pricing.

Business software, vertical SaaS, and product-led growth tools
