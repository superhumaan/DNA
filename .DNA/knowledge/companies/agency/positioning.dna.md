# Agency Archetype

## Delivery
- SOW → milestones → deliverables
- Client approval gates
- Time tracking (Harvest, Toggl, etc.)
- Industry rotates month-to-month — run `dna plan industry <sector>` per engagement

## Adaptive design systems
- **Client brand wins** on customer-facing surfaces when a style guide exists
- **DNA defaults** (MUI, list/report patterns) for internal admin and greenfield without brand
- Load `industries/<sector>/ui-patterns.dna.md` when `industry.active` is set
- Never mix two client design systems in one codebase — branch or theme per engagement

## AI tone
Client-readable language in external docs. Internal tickets can be technical.
Flag compliance and scope traps from industry agency-notes before estimating.
